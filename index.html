How I Work With You

A proven process that delivers consistent results for every client

01

Discovery & Audit

I start with a deep dive into your business, competitors, and current marketing performance. I analyze what's working, what's not, and identify the biggest opportunities for growth. This includes a comprehensive audit of your website, analytics, and existing campaigns.

02

Strategy Development

Based on the audit findings, I develop a custom strategy tailored to your goals, budget, and timeline. You'll receive a clear roadmap with prioritized actions, expected outcomes, and key milestones. No cookie-cutter plans. Every strategy is built from scratch for your specific situation.

03

Implementation

With your approval, I execute the strategy systematically. Whether it's technical SEO fixes, campaign launches, or content creation, every action is documented and tracked. You'll have full visibility into what's being done and why.

Marketing is never "set and forget." I continuously monitor performance, run tests, and optimize based on real data. What works gets scaled, what doesn't gets fixed or replaced. This iterative approach ensures consistent improvement over time.

05

Reporting & Communication

You'll receive regular reports that focus on metrics that actually matter like revenue, leads, and conversions, not vanity metrics. Monthly strategy calls ensure you understand exactly what's driving results. No jargon, no fluff, just clear insights.

I set realistic expectations upfront and provide monthly progress reports so you can track improvements along the way.

For Facebook/Instagram Ads, $1,500-2,000 monthly is a reasonable starting point. Management fees are separate from ad spend. That said, I'm happy to discuss your specific situation. Sometimes a smaller, focused campaign can work well for local businesses or niche markets.
